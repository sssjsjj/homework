1915.11.25~ 1930’s
1915 .11. 25Born in Asan-ri, Songjon-myeon, Tongcheon-gun, Gangwon-do
1930. 03 Graduated from Songjon Primary School
1934 Found employment at the Bok Heung Rice Store
1938. 01 Opened Kyeong Il Rice Store for business
1939. 01 Married Byun, Joong Seok
1940’s
1940. 03Established A-do Service, a joint-stock auto-repair company
1946. 04 Established Hyundai Auto Service
1947. 05 Established Hyundai Civil Works Company
1950’s
1950. 01 Merged Hyundai Auto Service with Hyundai Civil Works Company to establish Hyundai Engineering & Construction, Established Hyundai Engineering & Construction Company
1950. 03 Established Hyundai Transportation Company
1953. 04 Started the restoration works for Goryeong Bridge at Nakdong River (completed in May 1955)
1957. 09 Obtained an order to restore the Han River Footbridge, the single largest construction project since the Korean War
1960’s
1962. 07 Started construction work on the Danyang Cement Plant
1965. 09 Advanced into the overseas construction market for the first time in Korea, Obtained an order to construct the Pattani-Narathiwat Highway in Thailand
1967. 04 Started construction on the multipurpose dam in Soyang River (completed in December 1973)
1967. 12 Established Hyundai Motor Company
1968. 02 Commenced construction of the Gyeongbu Highway (later renamed: Gyeongbu Expressway)
1969. 01 Appointed as the chairman of the local social education supporters group in Korea
1970’s
1970. 01 Established Hyundai Cement Co., Ltd.
1970. 10 Started construction of the Gori Nuclear Power Plants No. 1
1971. 02 Appointed as the President of Hyundai Group
1971. 06 Established Keumgang Development Company
1972. 03 Groundbreaking ceremony of Hyundai Ulsan Shipyard
1973. 12 Established Hyundai Heavy Industries Company
1974. 02 Established Hyundai Engineering, Established Hyundai Motor Service
1975. 04 Established Hyundai Mipo Dockyard Co., Ltd.
1976. 02 Obtained an order to construct the Al-Jubayl Industrial Port in Saudi Arabia
1976. 03 Established the Asia Merchant Marine Company (the former Hyundai Merchant Marine Company)
1976. 12 Established Hyundai Corporation
1977. 02 Appointed as the chairman of the Federation of Korean Industries (FKI) (served 5 consecutive terms until 1987), Appointed as the chairman of the board of the Ulsan Technical High School
1977. 07 Established Asan Foundation, appointed as the chairman of the board, Established Hyundai Precision & Industry. Co., Ltd.
1978. 08 Started reclamation work at Cheonsu Bay in Seosan
1980’s
1981. 05 Appointed as the head of the Seoul Olympics Bidding Committee
1982. 07 Appointed the president of the Korean Olympic Committee
1983. 02 Established Hyundai Electronics Industries Co., Ltd.
1983. 09 Acquisition of Dongbang Marine & Fire Insurance (Formerly, Hyundai Marine & Fire Insurance Co., Ltd.)
1984. 02 Cofferdam work at Seosan reclamation project (attempted the so-called ‘Chung, Ju-yung engineering’ with an oil tanker)
1986. 11 Established Hyundai Development Company
1987. 02 Appointed as Honorary Chairman of the Hyundai Group, Appointed as Honorary Chairman of the Federation of Korean Industries (FKI)
1987. 09 Appointed as Inaugural Chairman of the Sejong Institute
1989. 07 Appointed as the Chairman of the Korea-USSR Economic Association
1990’s
1992. 01 Formed the United People’s Party
1992. 03 Elected as a member of the 14th National Assembly (Nationwide Proportional Seat)
1992. 12 Ran for presidency
1998. 06 Visited North Korea with 500 heads of cattle
1998. 10 Visited North Korea with 501 heads of cattle and met the leader Kim Jong-il
1998. 12 Acquisition of Kia Motors
1999. 02 Established Hyundai Asan Corporation
